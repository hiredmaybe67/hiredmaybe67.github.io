# Prestige Trap & How to Break Free
<p>Stop chasing other Peoples Goals</p>

In computer science theres this toxic idea that the pinnacle of success and status is an offer at a FAANG company.

## Whats the Cost? 

This is where it gets dangerous. Because once you start thinking like that, your goals stop being yours.

You’re not choosing paths based on what you want, you’re choosing based on what sounds impressive when you say it out loud.

And the worst part is, it *works*.  
People will be impressed.  
You’ll get validation.

If your decisions are driven by external approval, you end up optimizing for optics instead of reality. You pick roles that look good, not ones that feel right. And that’s how you wake up one day in a job you worked so hard to get… and realize you don’t even like it.

If you're going to work *So* hard for a job, why not the one you truly want. I'm guilty of doing it too. Its disgusting how much time I wasted working on something I didn't even want.
